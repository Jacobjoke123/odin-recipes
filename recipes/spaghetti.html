Spaghetti & Meatballs

Description

This dish is my personal spaghetti and meatballs recipe I make at home.

Ingredients

  • Dried Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 6 pre-cooked frozen meatballs

Steps

  1. Open can of tomatoes
  2. Over medium heat, dump the tomatoes into a skillet, and add in the meatballs
  3. Set a sauce pan full of pasta over high heat to boil.
  4. After 20 minutes, begin cooking the pasta.
  5. Once pasta is cooked to preference, drain and serve.
